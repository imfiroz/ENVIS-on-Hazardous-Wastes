ICMGP 2015: 12th International Conference on Mercury as a Global Pollutant

The International Conference on Mercury as a Global Pollutant (ICMGP), held periodically for over 20 years since its inception in 1990, has provided a forum for researchers and policy makers to explore important advances in mercury research and to facilitate internal and international collaborations. Recent meetings have attracted many hundreds to over a thousand conferees for a five day conference and exhibition. All contributions in forms of oral presentations and posters are very welcomed as well as participations in the exhibition.

  6. Remediation and control technologies
  7. Mercury contaminated sites
  8. Mercury in artisanal and small scale gold mining
  9. Environmental Biogeochemistry: Field Approaches

  11. Mercury bioaccumulation and trophic transfer
  12. Mercury exposure and effects in fish and wildlife
  13. Mercury in marine ecosystems
  14. Mercury in Polar ecosystems
  15. Mercury and climate change
  16. Human exposure and risk assessment
  17. Health effects of mercury
  18. Mercury containing wastes
  19. Supply and trade issues: Product, commodityand waste
  20. Mercury regulatory issues and policy
  21. Socio and economic aspects of mercury issues
  22. Implementation activities of Minamata convention on mercury
